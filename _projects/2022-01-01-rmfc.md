<h1>A Look at Reciprocal Multifactorial Constants</h1>

<p>This project is an in-depth analysis of a special set of infinite series called the reciprocal multifactorial series. We define it, prove a general closed form formula for the series, derive asymptotic approximations for the constants, and even prove a closed form formula for a Generalized Reciprocal Multifactorial Series.</p>
