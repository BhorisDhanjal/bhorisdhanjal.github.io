<h1>Calculating mathematical constants using Monte Carlo simulations</h1>

<p>This project primarily serves to showcase Monte Carlo simulations. We first derive the naïve Monte Carlo estimator for numerical integration. Following which we use Monte Carlo simulations to estimate a few well known mathematical constants. Variance reduction techniques and Quasi-Monte Carlo integration will be examined at the end.</p>
